Kira Davies is a collaborative storyteller who explores multimedia through live performance. She studied cinematography at the School of Visual Arts.

She directed *13 Kushaqua Trail*, an experimental silent movement film exploring variable audio composition, which was installed at the *Outlet Dance Festival*.
